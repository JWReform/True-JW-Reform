Core Beliefs and Differences with the Watchtower

The core beliefs are basically the same (no Trinity, no hellfire, importance of Jehovah's Name, earthly paradise) but we reject:

  • We believe that each congregation should be able to elect its own elders, rejecting central control.
  • We reject the claim that in 1919, Christ chose the governing body as His mouthpiece.
  • We believe all Christians should have the heavenly hope, and the 144,000 is symbolic, not literal.
  • We reject certain doctrines and embrace only that which aligns with scripture.
